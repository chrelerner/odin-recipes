Berried Delight

Description

Glazed strawberries crown a fluffy cream cheese filling in a graham cracker crust. Top each slice with a dollop of whipped cream or whipped topping for a delicious dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 package package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened.
  • 1/3 cup confectioners' sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 container reduced-fat whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 graham cracker crust
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup strawberry glaze.

Steps

  1. Beat cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and lemon juice together in a bowl with an electric mixer on low speed.
  2. Add whipped topping and beat until filling is well-combined. Spoon filling into graham cracker crust.
  3. Stir strawberries and glaze together in a bowl until berries are coated; lay berries over pie filling.
  4. Refridgerate pie until chilled, at least 1 hour.
